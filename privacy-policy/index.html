Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the BYTE BRIEF app for mobile devices created by Minori Umetsu as a Free service.

Information Collection and Use

The Application fetches publicly available RSS feeds from third-party news sources. The Application does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to the developer.

Third Party Access

The Application retrieves publicly available content from the following news sources via RSS: ITmedia, ZDNet Japan, ASCII.jp, Gigazine, TechCrunch Japan, TechCrunch, Wired, Ars Technica, Hacker News. These services have their own privacy policies.

Opt-Out Rights

You can stop all collection of information by the Application by uninstalling it.
